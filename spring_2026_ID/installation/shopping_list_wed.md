## Shopping List by Stop

### Stop 1 — Azko (formerly Ace Hardware)

Nearest branch: Azko Kuningan/Setiabudi, Jl. Prof. Dr. Satrio (~5-15 min from PMI). Confirm at azko.id. Alternative: Azko Pancoran, Jl. Raya Kalibata No. 1 (~15 min south).

Fixed prices, no bargaining. Opens ~09:00.

| Item | Indonesian term | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| Stud finder | — | Not available in Glodok |
| Spray paint | — | Pylox, Samurai brands |
| GE2 silicone sealant | "silikon serbaguna netral" | GE2 not sold in Indonesia. Buy **Sika Sikasil-111** (neutral cure, outdoor, UV stable) |
| 30 ft extension cord | — | Branded, proper grounding |
| Cable clips for ceiling tacking | "klem kabel" / "klip kabel" | P-clips or nail-in cable clamps |

| Rubber washers | "ring karet" | Assortment pack |
| Concrete anchors | "fisher" | Fischer brand |
| Wood screws / cabinet screws | "sekrup kayu" | — |

## Bargaining Tips (Glodok / Mangga Dua / Blustru)

- Start at 60-70% of quoted price for hardware items
- Buying multiple items from one stall gives leverage — consolidate
- **Bring cash (Rupiah)** — many kiosks don't accept cards
- ATMs at Harco Glodok Floor 6
- Azko is fixed-price (no bargaining)
