================================================================================
    VOLTAGE SUMMARY                  FUSE / PROTECTION SUMMARY
================================================================================
    Solar system:   12V nom          PoE circuit:   5A inline fuse
    Witty Pi in:    12V (5-26V ok)   Witty Pi:      internal protection
    Pi 5 (USB-C):   5V              Pi USB ports:   built-in OCP
    PoE injector:   12V in/48V out   LEDs:          330 Ohm series R
    Camera (PoE):  48V               Button GPIO:   internal pull-up
    GPIO signals:   3.3V
    LTE modem:      5V (USB bus)
================================================================================
    WIRE COLORS: RED = +12V  |  BLACK = GND  |  BLUE = DATA  |  GREEN = GPIO
    WIRE GAUGE:  18 AWG (power)  |  22 AWG (signal)  |  Cat6 (Ethernet)
================================================================================
    POWER BUDGET:  Peak ~42W (3.5A @ 12V)  |  Sleep ~0.6W (50mA @ 12V)
    CYCLE: 2.5 min ON / 12.5 min SLEEP  (96 cycles/day)
================================================================================

    NOTES:
    1. PoE injector powered via Witty Pi switched 12V rail - camera
       power-cycles in sync with Pi wake/sleep schedule.
    2. No heaters installed (Sukabumi tropical climate, power budget).
    3. Conformal coating MG 422C applied to all exposed PCBs.
    4. Dielectric grease on all outdoor electrical connections.
    5. Gore vent on enclosure for pressure equalization.
    6. Carrier: Telkomsel (Indonesia).
