# Sites for ORC deployment in Indonesia 2026


## Sukabumi Site

An existing site where a combined compute / video unit failed due to trapped humidity. The site is solar with no easily accessible utility power.

Equipment for this site:

 - Mounting pole (already installed)
 - 200W of solar panels (already installed)
 - Solar charge controller (already installed)
 - 50ah lifepo battery (already installed)

 - Raspberry pi
 - Witty 4 + battery for power management
 - USB storage
 - LTE modem, antenna and SMA bulkhead
 - Modbus interface for accepting logs from other devices
 - Small enclosure / mounting for pi and related hardware within larger power enclosure (suitable for tropics)
 - (optional) External rain gauge


 - Dual Gore vent camera enclosures
 - Dual USB cameras (IMX708 or equiv)
 - Dessicant packs
 - IR illuminator
 - Relay for controlling IR illuminator based on Pi's duty cycle




## Jakarta Site

This will be a new site that is used for training and testing purposes. We will be using some new approaches here, including setting up a site running on AC power with battery backup. No equipment exists at this site yet.

 - Mounting pole
 - Power enclosure (with some kind of active cooling) with pole mount
 - Power supply suitable for 110/220v utility power
 - Surge suppressor / power conditioner to deal with dirty power
 - UPS system to supply 24 hours' emergency power


 - Raspberry pi
 - Witty 4 + battery for power management
 - USB storage
 - LTE modem, antenna and SMA bulkhead
 - 12v PoE switch
 - Modbus interface for accepting logs from other devices
 - Small enclosure / mounting for pi and related hardware within larger power enclosure (suitable for tropics)
 - (optional) External rain gauge

 - 2 factory sealed PoE security cameras (8MP minimum) with IR illumination up to 30 meters
 - Pole mounts for cameras
