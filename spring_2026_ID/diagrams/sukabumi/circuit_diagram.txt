    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    UART (GPIO 14 TX, GPIO 15 RX) - RAIN GAUGE
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    Hydreon RG-15 (solid-state optical rain gauge)
      │
      ├── VCC ← 12V from TB1 (direct, no regulator)
      ├── GND ← TB1 GND
      ├── TX  → GPIO 15 (Pi UART RX)     RS232 TTL 3.3V
      └── RX  ← GPIO 14 (Pi UART TX)     (through PG9 cable gland)

    NOTE: RG-15 powered at 12V (7-24V range), signal at 3.3V TTL.
    No level shifter needed. Resolution: 0.2mm (configurable).
    Self-cleaning lens, no moving parts.
    Mounted outside on pole arm, built-in mounting holes.

    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    Quectel EG25-G (via EXVIST Mini PCIe-USB adapter)
      │
      ├── U.FL Main ──► SMA pigtail ──► Proxicast ANT-122-S02
      │                                 2x2 MIMO LTE Puck Antenna
      └── U.FL Aux  ──► SMA pigtail ──┘ (IP67, 12mm hole mount
                                          on enclosure roof)

    NOTE: Single sealed puck antenna with Main + Diversity ports.
    12mm hole drilled in enclosure top, puck screws down with
    rubber gasket. No SMA bulkhead connectors needed.
    Internal SMA cables route to modem U.FL connectors.
    Frequency: 600-6000 MHz (all Indonesian LTE bands).

    POWER BUDGET (per cycle):
      Active (~2.5 min):  Pi 5 15W + Modem 5W + PoE camera 12W + misc 2W = ~34W
      Sleep (~12.5 min):  Pi halt state ~1.5W
      Daily energy: ~166 Wh/day  (within 200W panel / 50Ah battery capacity)
      Battery autonomy: ~3.6 days (no sun)

    NOTES:
    1. PoE switch powered via relay triggered by Pi USB power -
       camera power-cycles in sync with Pi wake/sleep schedule.
       Pi ON = relay closed = PoE switch ON = camera ON.
    2. DDR-60G converters provide regulated voltage from battery
       (which varies 10-14V depending on charge state).
    3. No heaters installed (Sukabumi tropical foothills climate,
       solar power budget constraint).
    4. Conformal coating MG 422C applied to all exposed PCBs
       (Pi 5, Geekworm G469). Mask connectors.
    5. Dielectric grease on all outdoor electrical connections.
    6. Gore M12 vent on enclosure for pressure equalization.
    7. Carrier: Telkomsel (Indonesia, best rural coverage).
    8. Pi 5 built-in RTC with ML-2020 coin cell provides
       scheduling via Rainbow Sensing image.
    9. Samsung FIT Plus USB drive is IP67 waterproof, plugs
       directly into Pi USB-A 3.0 port.
   10. SHT40 mounted inside enclosure for climate monitoring.
       DS18B20 probe routed outside for ambient/dew point calc.
